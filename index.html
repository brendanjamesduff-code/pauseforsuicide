Reflection Board

You are not alone. Others have left notes here — not for advice, but to be with you in this moment. Read gently.

  • “I’m still here. One slow breath at a time.”

  • “Called a friend today. It helped.”

  • “I don’t know what tomorrow brings, but I’m willing to see it.”

Notes are moderated for care and safety. No names required.

Helpful Places

If you’re feeling isolated or thinking about suicide, these may help. Pick one small step.

  • Emergency (UK & Ireland)
    If you’re in immediate danger: call 999 / 112.
  • Samaritans (UK & ROI)
    24/7 listening support. Call 116 123 or visit samaritans.org.
  • Befrienders Worldwide
    Find help in many countries: befrienders.org.
  • Grounding Music & Books
    (You’ll add your curated list here — songs, playlists, books that steady the breath.)
